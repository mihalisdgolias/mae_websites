MSc in Management and AI: Entrepreneurship and Digital Transformation

Department of Management, Entrepreneurship and Digital Enterprise · School of Tourism Management, Hospitality and Entrepreneurship · Cyprus University of Technology

Overview

The MSc in Management and AI: Entrepreneurship and Digital Transformation is a postgraduate programme of the Cyprus University of Technology, offered by the Department of Management, Entrepreneurship and Digital Enterprise within the School of Tourism Management, Hospitality and Entrepreneurship. The programme combines the study of management with the application of artificial intelligence and digital technologies, and is organised around two concentrations: Entrepreneurship and Digital Transformation.

As its title indicates, the programme is intended for graduates and professionals who wish to develop and lead ventures, and to manage technology-driven change within organisations. The Entrepreneurship concentration is oriented towards the creation, financing and growth of new ventures, while the Digital Transformation concentration is oriented towards the adoption of digital and AI-enabled technologies to redesign organisational processes, products and business models.

Knowledge

On successful completion of the programme, graduates would be expected to be able to:

  • Demonstrate an advanced understanding of management concepts and their application in entrepreneurial and digitally transforming organisations.
  • Explain the role of artificial intelligence and digital technologies in contemporary management and business decision-making.
  • Describe the processes involved in creating, financing and growing new ventures (Entrepreneurship concentration).
  • Describe the principles and processes of digital transformation in organisations (Digital Transformation concentration).

Skills

On successful completion of the programme, graduates would be expected to be able to:

  • Apply management and analytical methods to entrepreneurial and digital-transformation problems.
  • Evaluate opportunities for new ventures and for the adoption of AI-enabled and digital technologies.
  • Design and assess plans for launching ventures and for managing technology-driven organisational change.
  • Communicate analyses, recommendations and decisions to specialist and non-specialist audiences.

Competences

On successful completion of the programme, graduates would be expected to be able to:

  • Act autonomously and responsibly in managing entrepreneurial and digital-transformation initiatives.
  • Lead and work within teams undertaking innovation and change in organisations.
  • Make and justify decisions in complex and uncertain business environments, taking ethical and societal considerations into account.
  • Continue learning independently in a rapidly evolving technological landscape.

Career Prospects

The programme's two concentrations point to a range of career directions. Graduates of the Entrepreneurship concentration would typically pursue roles in founding and growing new ventures, as well as roles supporting entrepreneurship and innovation within established organisations. Graduates of the Digital Transformation concentration would typically pursue roles in leading and managing technology-driven change, including the adoption of AI-enabled and digital technologies in business. More generally, the programme is oriented towards AI-enabled management roles across sectors.
